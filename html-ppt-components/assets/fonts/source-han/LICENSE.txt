Wise Han Sans SC and Wise Han Serif SC are site-specific subset webfonts
derived from Source Han Sans SC and Source Han Serif SC, which are distributed
under the SIL Open Font License, Version 1.1.

Source Han Sans:
https://github.com/adobe-fonts/source-han-sans

Source Han Serif:
https://github.com/adobe-fonts/source-han-serif

The reserved font name is "Source". Because these webfonts are subset for the
site's current character set, their family names are changed to Wise Han Sans SC
and Wise Han Serif SC.

See the canonical license text:
https://github.com/adobe-fonts/source-han-sans/blob/master/LICENSE.txt
https://github.com/adobe-fonts/source-han-serif/blob/master/LICENSE.txt
